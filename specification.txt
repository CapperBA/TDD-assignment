Title: 
Grade Converter

Usage: 
Grade Converter is an app that takes a danish grade (7-step scale) as input
and converts it to the european standard (ECTS)

Specifications:
Danish grades are equal to the following in the european standards:
12 = A
10 = B
 7 = C
 4 = D
02 = E
00 = Fx
-3 = F
